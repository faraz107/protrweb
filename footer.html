To cite protr / ProtrWeb in a publication, use:
Nan Xiao, Dong-Sheng Cao, Min-Feng Zhu, and Qing-Song Xu. (2015). protr/ProtrWeb: R package and web server for generating various numerical representation schemes of protein sequences. Bioinformatics 31, 1857-1859.
The ProtrWeb app is developed and maintained by Nan Xiao.
ProtrWeb has been tested under IE9, IE10, Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox and Apple Safari.
